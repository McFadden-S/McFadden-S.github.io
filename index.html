Shae McFadden

PhD Student

Dept. of Informatics

King's College London

Currently a Ph.D. candidate, supervised by Dr. Fabio Pierazzi, at King’s College London, investigating the applications of deep reinforcement learning to cybersecurity. A passion for the cross-section between artificial intelligence and cybersecurity resulted in collaborations with the Systems Security Research Lab (S2Lab) at UCL and the AI for Cyber Defence Research Centre (AICD) at The Alan Turing Institute. Graduated from King’s College London with a first-class B.Sc. (Hons) in Computer Science specialising in Artificial Intelligence. During undergraduate studies, was awarded the Layton Research Award, the Alan Fairbourn Memorial Prize, and the Associateship of King’s College.
